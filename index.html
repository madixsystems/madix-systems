Who is Madix Systems for?+

Business owners, independent professionals, and small teams that need clearer lead follow-up, CRM organization, client communication, onboarding, reporting, retention, or operating workflows.

Do I need GoHighLevel?+

No. Madix can begin with the tools you already use, including spreadsheets, email, HubSpot, Zoho, Podio, or another CRM. A new platform should be recommended only when the workflow truly requires it.

What happens after I request the playbook?+

After the form is connected, your information is sent securely through Formspree and the download link appears. You may also receive occasional practical emails from Madix Systems.

Are the three memberships charging me now?+

No. The current buttons join a founding-member waitlist. They do not ask for payment details and they do not activate a subscription.

What can May help organize?+

Lead stages, follow-up ownership, customer communication, appointment setting, onboarding, CRM fields, task handoffs, simple automations, dashboards, SOPs, documentation, and retention workflows.

How are reviews published?+

A former client submits the review form and grants permission. May verifies the relationship, reviews the wording for accuracy, and then adds the approved review. Reviews do not publish automatically.

Why are large brand names shown?+

They describe past support programs, work environments, or tools used. They do not imply that those brands sponsor, endorse, or currently employ Madix Systems.

What if I am not ready to hire anyone?+

Start with the free playbook. Use it to identify one costly gap and one practical next action. You can contact Madix when the problem and desired result are clearer.
